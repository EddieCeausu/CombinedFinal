Charles McClung McGhee


Thesis

Charles McClung McGhee did not build empires, nor was he an innovator, or a national major financer. However, through his pre-Civil War accumulated wealth from a pork packing plant, and his resourceful business strategies after the War, McGhee rose to become one of the most successful Southern Businessmen in the late 19th century.
